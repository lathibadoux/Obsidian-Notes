1. Data analysts / Data scientists
	* Should be very easy to query
	* Not many complex data types
	* Very flat
	* a.k.a OLAP cube
	* Structs and arrays are not okay
2. Other data engineers
	* Should be compact and probably harder to query
	* Nested types are okay
	* "Master data" - many pipelines read from your data and create other data sets
	* Structs and arrays are okay
3. ML models
	* Depends on the model and how its trained
	* Want an identifier and then a bunch of flattened decimal columns
4. Customers
	* Should be a very easy to interpret chart
